About Attendance Management System

Our Attendance Management System offers an efficient and modern solution for tracking student attendance. Students can easily scan their ID cards to mark their "In" and "Out" attendance automatically, removing the need for manual entry.

With a secure login system, each student can access their personal attendance dashboard and view their attendance graph across the semester, helping them monitor and improve their presence consistently.

Additionally, teachers have the ability to access and review the attendance records of all students in a visually appealing graphical format. This ensures easier management, quick insights, and better decision-making for academic performance tracking.

Our system ensures accuracy, transparency, and convenience by combining technology and user-friendly design, revolutionizing the traditional attendance process.
